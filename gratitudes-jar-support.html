Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I use Gratitudes Jar?
Open the app, write a gratitude note, and drop it into the jar. Shake your phone to see a random gratitude note from the jar.

2. Is the app free?
Yes, the Gratitudes Jar app is free to use.

Troubleshooting

If the app is not working as expected, try restarting your device. If the issue persists, contact us for further assistance.
